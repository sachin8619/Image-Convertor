Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Image Converter application processes images locally on your device using browser technologies. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or images you upload.

Data Usage

All image processing (e.g., conversion, resizing) is performed client-side. No data is sent to our servers or third parties.

Cookies

This site does not use cookies or any form of tracking.

About Us

Welcome to Image Converter, a simple and free tool designed to help you convert and resize images directly in your browser.

Our mission is to provide a fast, secure, and user-friendly experience without compromising your privacy. All processing happens locally, ensuring your images stay safe.
